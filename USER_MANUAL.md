## 1. Getting Started

### Registration & Email Verification (OTP)
To start using the platform, you need to create a secure account.
1. Navigate to the **Sign Up** page.
2. Enter your full name, email address, and a secure password.
3. Click **Register**.
4. **Email OTP Verification:** Check your email inbox. You will receive a 6-digit One-Time Password (OTP).
5. Enter the OTP on the verification screen to confirm your email address and activate your account.

### Login
1. Go to the **Login** page.
2. Enter your registered email and password.
3. Once authenticated, you will be directed to your dashboard.

### Onboarding Profile
If this is your first time logging in, you will be guided through a quick **Onboarding** process:
* **Financial Goals:** Select your primary financial goals (e.g., saving for a house, paying off debt, building an emergency fund).
* **Income & Assets:** Provide a rough estimate of your current income and assets so the AI can offer better tailored advice.

## 2. Dashboard Overview

Your **Dashboard** is the central hub for your financial health. Here you will find:
* **Quick Metrics:** At-a-glance summaries of your total balance, monthly spending, and budget adherence.
* **Recent Activity:** A quick view of your latest transactions.
* **Navigation Sidebar:** Quick links to all other modules including Chat, Analytics, Budgets, and Transactions.

## 3. Managing Your Finances

### Transactions
The **Transactions** page allows you to keep track of your cash flow.
* **Add a Transaction:** Click the "Add Transaction" button. Enter the amount, select the category (e.g., Groceries, Rent, Salary), choose whether it's an Income or Expense, and add a brief description.
* **View History:** See a chronological list of all your past transactions.

### Budgets
Set spending limits to keep your finances on track.
* **Create a Budget:** Go to the **Budgets** page, select a category, and set a monthly limit.
* **Track Progress:** Visual progress bars will show you how close you are to reaching your budget limits based on your logged transactions.

## 4. AI Financial Advisor (Chat)

The standout feature of this platform is your personal **AI Financial Agent**, powered by OpenAI GPT-4o.
* **Accessing the Chat:** Click on the **Chat** icon in the sidebar.
* **What you can ask:** The AI has persistent memory of your financial profile. You can ask questions like:
  * *"How can I reduce my grocery spending this month?"*
  * *"Based on my goals, how much should I put into my emergency fund?"*
  * *"What is a good strategy to pay off my current debts?"*
* **Context Boundary:** The AI is strictly programmed to provide advice only on financial topics. It will refuse to answer non-financial queries to ensure focus and reliability.

* **Profile Settings:** Update your personal information, adjust your financial goals, or modify your notification preferences.

## 7. Troubleshooting & Support

* **Didn't receive the OTP?** Check your spam/junk folder. Ensure that the email provided during registration is correct. You can click "Resend OTP" on the verification screen.
* **AI Advisor not responding?** Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If you are hosting locally, verify that your `OPENAI_API_KEY` is correctly set in your `.env` file.
* **Forgot Password?** Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to securely reset your credentials via your registered email.
